Chinese New Year Dragon City Run

Hosted By
Jason L.

Details

Join Bangkok Runners for an exciting Chinese New Year City Run where you can discover the beauty of the outdoors within the Old Bangkok City while getting fit and healthy!

Join us on 10th Feb at Sam Yot MRT Station for this Fun Run. We will be running at around 6.30min/km pace but a a few stops for photos and water.
Total Distance: 13km

Some of the Places of interests you will be running by
1- Rommaninat Park - Previously a prison
2. Democracy Monument
3. Khao San Road
4. Sumen Fort - 1 of the 2 remaining forts from the early Rattanakosin
5. Rama VIII Bridge
6. Chao Phraya River
7. Sky Park
8. Grand Palace
9. Temple of the reclining buddha (Wat Po)
10. Temple of Dawn (Wat Arun)
11. Flower Market

We will move off exactly at 7am so please be early.

COVID-19 safety measures

Event will be outdoor
